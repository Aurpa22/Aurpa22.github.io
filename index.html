I serve as the founding Lecturer and Chairperson of the Department of Data Science and Engineering at the University of Frontier Technology, Bangladesh. My deep passion for research and teaching, with a concentration on Natural Language Processing (NLP), Data Science, and Deep Learning, has driven me to pursue this career. I maintain a strong research profile, with over 20 publications in reputable journals and conferences, and have been honored with consecutive University Grants Commission (UGC) research grants.

In addition to my instructional and leadership roles, I am dedicated to mentoring undergraduate students in their research projects and theses, contributing to their academic growth and practical skill development. My experience as the founding chairperson has equipped me with strong capabilities in academic administration, program development, and fostering a collaborative learning environment.
